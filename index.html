Help

This application is for authorized clinic or hospital staff only. Patients do not log in, enter symptoms, or receive recommendations directly from the app.

How clerking works

  1. Select or create a patient
  2. Start an encounter
  3. Enter the presenting complaint
  4. Confirm symptoms
  5. Generate follow-up questions
  6. Record answers
  7. Analyze findings
  8. Review and approve the plan

Important safety notes

  • Generated condition candidates and plans require clinician verification.
  • The app does not make a final diagnosis or prescribe independently.
  • Records are stored only in this browser (local storage on this device).
  • Emergency escalation follows your facility’s normal procedures.
  • Download a data backup before clearing browser data or changing the site address.

Use Settings to download or restore backups. Do not commit patient records to shared repositories.
